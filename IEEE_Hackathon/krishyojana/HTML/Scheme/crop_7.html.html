National Programme on Organic Production

Development of organic value chains in the North East Region of India and promote organic farming.

Important Information:

  • Among states, MP has covered the largest area under organic certification followed by Rajasthan, Maharashtra and UP.
  • During 2016, Sikkim had achieved a remarkable distinction of converting its entire cultivable land (more than 76,000 hectares) under organic certification.
